### Recording a Hole (Step by Step)

1. **Select Par** (Par 3, 4, or 5)
2. **Enter Distance to Hole** (in yards) - *Optional but recommended*
3. **Fairway?** (Yes/No) - *Auto-skipped on Par 3s*
4. **Green in Regulation?** (Yes/No)
5. **If missed GIR:**
   - **Missed Green Direction** (Short/Long/Left/Right/combinations)
   - **Chip Up & Down?** (Yes/No)
   - **If not chipped up:** Enter shots to green
6. **Approach Distance** (feet from hole when you start putting)
7. **Number of Putts** (0-10)
8. **Finish Putt Dist.** (feet - distance of final putt that went in)
9. **Penalty Stroke(s)** dropdown (if applicable)
10. **Sand Save** checkbox (if applicable)

**Score automatically calculates!** No manual entry needed.

**Time**: ~15 seconds per hole

## ğŸ“ Golf Stat Education

### What is GIR?
**Greens in Regulation**: Reaching the green in par minus 2 strokes
- Par 3: On in 1 shot
- Par 4: On in 2 shots
- Par 5: On in 3 shots

**Why it matters**: GIR is the #1 predictor of low scores

### What is Scrambling?
**Scrambling %**: (Up & Downs / Missed GIRs) Ã— 100

When you miss the green, do you still save par?
- **Tour Pros**: ~60%
- **Scratch golfer**: ~40-50%
- **Average**: ~20-30%

### What's a Good Scrambling %?
- **30%+**: Good
- **40%+**: Very good
- **50%+**: Excellent

### Fairway Stats
**Why track fairways?**
- Hitting fairway = easier approach
- Missing fairway = harder to hit GIR
- Fairways setup birdies

**Tour average**: ~60% fairways

### Fastest Data Entry:
1. Know your putts/score before opening app

3. Skip optional fields if pressed for time
4. Use voice assistant to count putts

### Pattern Recognition:
After 9 holes, check:
- Are you always missing greens short/long?
- Always missing fairways left/right?
- More 3-putts on long putts?

**These patterns = practice targets!**

### Practice Based on Data:
If app says "Poor scrambling cost you 3 strokes":
- Spend next range session on chipping
- Practice from 5-15 yards around green
- Work on different lies (rough, fringe, sand)
